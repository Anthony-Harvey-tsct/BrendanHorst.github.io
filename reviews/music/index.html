Is a gameboy an instrument?

Yes, yes it is. I don't just mean ost's to gameboy games, I mean songs that are composed on gameboy hardware, or use soundfonts similar to that of a gameboy. They're called chiptunes

One of my favorite chiptune albums is Parellel Processing by Danimal Cannon and Zef.

One of the common pitfalls of chiptune music is that songs from inexperienced artists can sound a little empty due to the limited hardware. While many artists get around this by adding extra instruments or synths, Parellel Processing avoids this by being mostly composed on two gameboys rather than just one. The result is an album jam packed with catchy melodies and hard-hitting basslines that most gameboy games from back in the day wished they had.
